Banana Pancakes

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • An Egg
  • Milk
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Bananas

How to Make Banana Pancakes

  1. Mix combine dry ingredients in one bowl and wet ingredients in another. Add dry ingredients to the bowl with wet ingredients, then stir until incorporated
  2. Cook-Pour batter in 1/4 cup portions onto lightly oiled pan or griddle over medium high heat. Cook for a few minutes, flip with spatula and cook on other side for a few minutes until golden brown
  3. Serve-Serve pancakes immediately
