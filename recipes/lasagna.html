Homemade Lasagna

Homemade lasagna isn't as hard to make as it seems. Don't be intimidated by all the layers of delicious Italian flavor â€” try this homemade lasagna recipe today!

Ingredients:

  • Meat: 1/2 pound ground pork, 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • Onion: Diced onion
  • Canned tomatoes: Can of tomato sauce and can of crushed tomatoes
  • Fresh Herbs: Two tablespoons chopped parsley
  • Sugar: Dash of sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings: Dried basil, dried oregano, salt, black pepper
  • Noodles: Uncooked or oven ready lasagna noodles
  • Cheese: Cottage cheese and parmasen and shredded mozzeralla
  • Eggs: For binding agent

Cooking Instruction

  1. Cook the meat Cook ground meat in skillet until browned and crumbly. Add onions and continue Cooking until it's translucent. Stir in canned tomato, half of the parseley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons salt, oregano and sugar
  2. Cook the noodles. Boil lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they&rsquore al dente
  3. Make the cheese layer. Mix cottage cheese, parm, Eggs remaining parsley, remaining salt and pepper in a bowl
  4. Assemble the lasagna Layer the ingredients according to the recipt (starting with sauce and ending with mozeralla) until the lasagna is assembled
  5. Bake the lasagna Cover with foil and bake in preheated oven for about half an hour. Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown

How Long to Cook Lasagna

In an oven preheated to 375 degrees F, this homemade lasagna should be perfectly baked in about 50 minutes (30-40 min covered, 5-10 min uncovered)
